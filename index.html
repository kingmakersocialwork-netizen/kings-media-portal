⚡ Week 1 Action Plan for KMS Members
1

Film Your Next Training Session

Go to the gym this week with your phone set up to record. You need:

  • One intense sparring or drilling round
  • One moment of tension between you and a partner
  • Your face after a hard round — no filter, no cleanup
2

Post With One of These Titles

Use the exact formats working right now:

  • "TENSION AT [your gym name]"
  • "I've been training [X] years for this"
  • "The [technique] nobody talks about — pt. 1"
  • "Gone wrong: I challenged [partner] and…"
3

Film Yourself Talking

Most underutilized format. Stand in front of a camera and say:

  • One hard truth about training
  • One story from your martial arts journey
  • One strong opinion about the sport
  • No script. 30–60 seconds. Done.
4

Find the Light in Your Gym

The 3.2M reel was a fighter just standing in good lighting. This week:

  • Find a corner with one strong light source
  • Film 10 seconds — no text, no music
  • Post it. Caption: your name. Gym. Nothing else.
5

Post to Both Platforms

Same content, two places. What works on one works on both. Remove TikTok watermark before crossposting to IG Reels.

  • TikTok: favor sound, trends, text overlay
  • Instagram: favor visuals, cinematic quality, captions
6

Bring It Into the Brotherhood

Post your content in the KMS chat when you publish. The brotherhood helps boost early engagement — which is what the algorithm needs in the first 30 minutes.

  • Share the reel in the group chat
  • Ask members to watch + comment
  • Comment on theirs back
